Personal Portfolio Website

A website designed as an attempt to learn HTML and CSS.

Summer 2023 Keiane Balicanta

This is a work in progress!

When it came to software, I always had a profienciency and nack for general purpose languages such as C, C++, and Java as well as being interested in learning Python one day. The thought of developing a website and front end web development never fell within my interest. The syntax is unfamiliar to what I am used to and I am not much of a creative person. However, I have seen enough people using personal websites to display their projects and portfolios and it looks really good on resumes. So as a way to stand out and learn something new, I have started taking free courses to teach myself HTML and CSS to hopefully fully develop this website.

As a start, because I am not an expert, I have used a template from HTML5 UP to get this started but with enough time and learning, I will plan to redesign this to my liking.
